## 3. SERVICES AND PRICING

### 3.1 Group Room Block

(a) Hotel agrees to set aside a block of guest rooms for Agency's tour groups during the dates specified in confirmed booking schedules. Each confirmed booking shall be documented as **Exhibit A — Booking Confirmation** and attached to this Agreement.

(b) Agency shall submit booking requests no fewer than **twenty-one (21) days** in advance of the scheduled arrival date. Each request shall specify:

- Number of rooms required by room type

## 5. CANCELLATION AND ATTRITION POLICY

### 5.1 Cancellation Deadlines and Fees (Per Booking)

| Notice Period (prior to scheduled arrival date) | Cancellation Fee |
|---|---|
| **More than 30 days** | No charge / Full refund of deposit |
| **14–30 days** | **[_____]%** of total room revenue (based on original room block) |
| **7–13 days** | **[_____]%** of total room revenue (based on original room block) |
| **Less than 7 days** | **100%** of total room revenue (based on original room block) |
| **No-show (no prior notice)** | **100%** of total room revenue (based on original room block) |

Total room revenue for cancellation purposes shall be calculated by multiplying the agreed group rate per room type by the number of room-nights in the canceled booking.

### 5.2 Attrition (Room Block Reduction)

(a) Agency may reduce the number of rooms in a confirmed block without penalty up to the following deadlines:

| Reduction Period | Allowable Reduction |
|---|---|
| Until **21 days** prior to arrival | Up to **[_____]%** of the room block without penalty |
| **14–20 days** prior to arrival | Up to **[_____]%** of the room block without penalty |
| **7–13 days** prior to arrival | Up to **[_____]%** of the room block without penalty |
| **Less than 7 days** prior to arrival | No reduction allowed; full block charged |

(b) Any reduction beyond the allowable percentages set forth above shall be treated as a cancellation of the excess rooms and shall incur cancellation fees per Section 5.1, applied pro-rata.

### 5.3 Early Departure

If an Agency client checks out prior to the scheduled departure date, Hotel shall charge an early departure fee equal to **one (1) night's** room rate at the agreed group rate (or **[____]%** of remaining room-nights), unless the early departure is due to a medical emergency or Force Majeure Event.

### 5.4 Cancellation by Hotel

If Hotel must cancel a confirmed booking (other than due to a Force Majeure Event), Hotel shall:

1. Provide written notice at the earliest possible time;
2. Use best efforts to relocate Agency's group to a comparable alternative hotel at or below the agreed group rate, and cover any reasonable cost differential for equivalent accommodations; and
3. If relocation is not feasible, provide a **full refund** of all deposits and pre-paid amounts.

### 7.2 Agency Insurance

Agency represents that it maintains appropriate general liability and professional liability insurance. Agency may provide evidence upon Hotel's reasonable request.

### 7.3 Guest Property and Liability

(a) Hotel shall exercise reasonable care in safeguarding guest property and baggage. Agency acknowledges that Hotel's liability for loss of or damage to guest property is governed by California Civil Code Section 1859 et seq. and any applicable Hotel policies posted in guest rooms and at the front desk.

(b) Agency shall recommend that each tour participant carry personal travel insurance covering trip cancellation, medical, and personal property.

### 7.4 Mutual Indemnification

(a) **Agency's Indemnity:** Agency shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Hotel, its affiliates, officers, directors, employees, and agents from third-party claims arising from (i) Agency's breach of this Agreement; (ii) Agency's negligent acts or omissions or willful misconduct; or (iii) any claim brought by an Agency client not directly caused by Hotel's gross negligence or willful misconduct.

(b) **Hotel's Indemnity:** Hotel shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Agency, its affiliates, officers, directors, employees, and agents from third-party claims arising from (i) Hotel's breach of this Agreement; (ii) Hotel's negligent acts or omissions or willful misconduct; or (iii) personal injury or property damage occurring on Hotel premises directly caused by Hotel's negligence.

### 7.5 Limitation of Liability

EXCEPT FOR INDEMNIFICATION OBLIGATIONS, BREACH OF CONFIDENTIALITY, OR WILLFUL MISCONDUCT, NEITHER PARTY SHALL BE LIABLE FOR INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES.

## 10. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 10.1 Term

This Agreement commences on the Agreement Date and shall continue for an initial term of **one (1) year** ("**Initial Term**"), automatically renewing for successive **one (1) year** renewal terms ("**Renewal Terms**"), unless either Party provides **sixty (60) days'** written notice of non-renewal.

### 10.2 Termination for Convenience

Either Party may terminate for any reason upon **ninety (90) days'** prior written notice. Confirmed bookings within the notice period shall be honored.

### 10.3 Termination for Cause

Either Party may terminate immediately upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within **fifteen (15) days** of written notice, or becomes insolvent or bankrupt.

### 10.4 Survival

Sections 5 (to the extent of outstanding cancellation fees), 6 (outstanding balances), 7 (Liability and Insurance), 9 (Confidentiality), 10.4 (Survival), and 12 (Governing Law) shall survive termination.

---

## 11. FORCE MAJEURE

Neither Party shall be liable for non-performance (other than payment obligations) caused by circumstances beyond its reasonable control, including: acts of God; fire; flood; earthquake; hurricane or severe weather; epidemic or pandemic (including related government restrictions); war; terrorism; civil unrest; labor strikes; or governmental action (each a "**Force Majeure Event**").

The affected Party shall provide prompt notice and use diligent efforts to resume performance. If a Force Majeure Event prevents a confirmed booking, the Parties shall negotiate in good faith to reschedule. If rescheduling is not feasible, all deposits and pre-payments shall be refunded in full within **fifteen (15) business days**.
