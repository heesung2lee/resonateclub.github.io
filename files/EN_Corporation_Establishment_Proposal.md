## 3. Business Description

Resonate Club is a **premium travel platform connecting Korea and the United States**. Initial focus is on Korea→USA golf tours, with plans to expand into USA→Korea inbound luxury travel upon upgrading to a comprehensive travel agency.

### A. Korea→USA Golf Tours (Outbound Golf)
- **Target**: Korean high-net-worth golfers with annual income of KRW 100M+
- **Key Courses**: Trump National LA, Sandpiper GC, Hidden Valley GC, and other premier California golf courses
- **Operating Model**: Small private groups of 2-8 persons with dedicated guide
- **Pricing**: Premium customized service-based pricing

### B. USA→Korea Inbound Luxury Tours (Future Expansion)
- **Target**: American luxury travelers with $150,000+ annual income
- **Key Content**: K-culture experiences, premium golf, Michelin fine dining
- **Operating Model**: To be launched after comprehensive travel agency upgrade (capital increase to KRW 100M + registration change)

## 7. Business Feasibility

### Market Perspective
- Korean golf population of **6.24 million**, overseas golf travel demand growing at 12.5% CAGR
- **1.3 million** US visitors to Korea annually, with surging K-culture-linked luxury inbound demand
- **Premium niche market** exists that large travel agencies have not adequately addressed

### Competitive Perspective
- Founder's **20+ years US residency, golf course hands-on experience, local network, and bicultural understanding** — an irreplaceable competitive advantage
- **Digital-first one-person corporation** model enables minimal fixed costs and high profitability
- Premium customized services provide a **predictable revenue structure**

### Financial Perspective
- Initial required funds of approximately KRW 67.7M — **ultra-low-cost structure**
- Annual fixed costs of approximately KRW 13.6M — **industry-leading low operating costs**
- Even under the conservative scenario (6 groups/year), Year 1 operating profit of approximately KRW 9.3M projected
- With 10 groups/year, net profit of approximately KRW 18.7M; additional growth upon comprehensive travel agency upgrade
